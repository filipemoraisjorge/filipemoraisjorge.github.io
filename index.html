I'm a Video Editor and Software Developer from Lisbon. I just concluded an intensive Java and JavaScript programming bootcamp at <Academia de Código_>.

With a 10 years Career in Media, I‘ve worked as director, which made me grow up in an industry where team work is vital. And as video editor my work had the same Pareto principle that coding has, 20% creativity, 80% organization. Through the years I’ve sharpened my eye and I’m now very picky about good design and creative originality.

As a kid I played by dismantling the toys and now, as an adult I’m still pretty curious about how things work, I just stopped calling it playing. I like old books, old cheap cameras, new music and new ideas for our society.

People tend to say I’m organized and creative, focused and methodical. I tend to say I feel they’re right.
